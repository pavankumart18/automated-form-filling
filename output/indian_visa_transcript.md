## Tutorial: indian_visa

**Step 1:** Open the Indian eVisa portal home page
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/tvoa.html

**Step 2:** Open the eVisa application form start page
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration

**Step 3:** Select nationality, passport type, and port of arrival
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration

**Step 4:** Fill date of birth and email confirmation
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration

**Step 5:** Choose visa service: eTourist Visa (30 Days)
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration

**Step 6:** Set expected date of arrival and acknowledge instructions
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration

**Step 7:** Stop at captcha step for safe, non-submitting demo
  - URL: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/Registration
